    📚 Variance Analysis Formulas

    Materials Variances

    Material Price Variance: (Actual Price - Standard Price) × Actual Quantity

    Material Usage Variance: (Actual Quantity - Standard Quantity) × Standard Price

    Labour Variances

    Labour Rate Variance: (Actual Rate - Standard Rate) × Actual Hours

    Labour Efficiency Variance: (Actual Hours - Standard Hours) × Standard Rate

    Overhead Variances

    Variable Overhead Variance: Actual Variable Overhead - (Standard Hours × Variable Rate)

    Fixed Overhead Volume Variance: (Standard Hours - Budgeted Hours) × Fixed Rate per Hour

    Sales Variances

    Sales Price Variance: (Actual Price - Standard Price) × Actual Volume

    Sales Volume Contribution Variance: (Actual Volume - Budgeted Volume) × Standard Contribution
